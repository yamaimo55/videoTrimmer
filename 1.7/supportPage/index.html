Welcome to VideoTrimmer!

You can trim, add or stretch the video by a specific number of seconds.

And you can change fps, bitrate, codec too.

How to trim

Step 1: Select video file from device (via Files app).


Step 2: TrimInput new video parameter, and trim video!

  • FPS
  • Select codec from H.264 or H.265 (HEVC)
  • Select auido & subtitle export
  • Bitrate(Mbit/s)
  • Input the seconds of trim or add for new video

To make more detailed edits, set the time and action on the Advanced page.

Step 2: StretchInput new video parameter, and stretch video!

  • Select codec from H.264 or H.265 (HEVC)
  • Export video only
  • Bitrate(Mbit/s)
  • Input the new total duration in seconds.
